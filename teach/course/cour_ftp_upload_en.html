Step by Step to use FTP
For Windows XP 、Windows 7 and Windows 8 Users
  1. In startup, please press the right button of mouse and click “Open Windows Explorer”


  2. In the internet address bar, please input ftp://, and press “Enter”. Please refer to the following picture.


  3. Then the system will prompt “Log On As dialog”
    Please enter your User name and Password.
    They are the same as your learning system account. And then click “Log On”.



  4. After you login successfully, The Windows Explorer lists your current content of this specified course. Please refer to the following.
PS.
  • You need to login your learning system first, use the “office environment”, switch to your target course, and click Courses Management > Upload Content

  • When you need to upload to another course, please close this Windows Explorer first. Then follow the same steps to update.
