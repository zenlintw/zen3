Follow the following format to edit your quiz, one line each.
Format
homework and exam : type,answer,question,choice,answer_detail,category (partitioned by comma, ",")
questionnaire : type,,question,choice,answer_detail,category (partitioned by comma, ",")
Illustration
  1. Type: shown as number, i.e., 1 for True/False Questions, 2 for Multiple-choice Questions, 3 for Multiple-answer Questions, 4 for Cloze Test, 5 for Short-answer Questions, and 6 for Matching Questions.
  2. Model answer:
    • True/False Questions: 1 stands for true and 0 for false.
    • Multiple-choice Questions: Answer with the number of choice, e.g., 2.
    • Multiple-answer Questions: Answer as above, but separate answers by a comma, e.g., 1,2,5.
    • Cloze Test: No need to fill in the blanks as the answers are shown in the questions. You'll figure it out when you scroll down.
    • Short-answer Questions: No model answers.
    • Matching Questions: Answer with a{2},b{1},c{3} to indicate that a matched 2, b matches 1, and c matched 3.
  3. Question description: Except for double colons ",", any characters can be acceptable. But in Cloze Test, the answers must be embraced by double parentheses, i.e., ((answer)).
  4. Choice (valid for either Multiple-choice Questions or Multiple-answer Questions): Every choice must be separated by " || " with "||" embraced by two spaces.
  5. Multiple-choice questions and matching questions, must """ (double quotes) around the whole answer block.
  6. When writing a question in English, due to the sentences of question description and choice(s) usually has a comma ",", so the question description and the choice(s) block should be marked with double quotes """.
  7. Matching Questions: Use two @s embraced by two spaces, i.e., " @@ ", to separate two group of choices, i.e., suggestions and choices. Keep empty for those question types without choices.
  8. answer_detail : It is the detail of answer description. The field can be ignore.
  9. category : It means the version, volume, chapter, paragraph and section. Please use dash sign (-) to combine them. example: 1-2-3-4-5. The field can be ignore or just use partial. example: -2--4-5.

Examples

    4,,"Ask (()) what your country can do for you, (()) what you can do for your country."
